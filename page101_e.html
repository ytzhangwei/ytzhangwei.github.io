Why the video can not be exported

There will be a failure when exporting the video.

There are several possible reasons for the failure, as below.

1. As the hardware configuration is low, can not transcoding high-definition format video (such as 1080P, 2K, 4K).

Solution: Reduce the exported resolution and then re-export.

2. Because the loaded effects are too much, the required memory exceeds the maximum of the App (Window store app memory limit depends on the physical memory of the device)

Solution: Reduce some effects or clips, divided into two or more times to complete the video editing work.

3. The system has a push update that is not installed.

Solution: Go to the "update and security" to install the latest updates, restart device and then open the App to export video again.

4. The imported video or audio format is not compatible.

Solution: Transcode video or audio to standard format via "Transcoding Tools".

If the above solution can not solve your problem, please email us to with details. We are very sorry for this, we will continue to optimize the App to reduce the probability of failure.
